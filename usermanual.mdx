The Investment Copilot is a powerful assistant designed to help individual investors optimize their portfolio through advanced AI-driven insights. This Copilot integrates a variety of tools, allowing for personalized analysis and solutions based on your specific portfolio and investment goals. With its AI-enhanced capabilities, you can go beyond traditional methods to receive dynamic, data-driven recommendations for managing and adjusting your investments.

Our experience has shown that the best results come from combining AI with expert financial knowledge. These tailored tools align with your unique objectives, enabling the Investment Copilot to effectively support your decision-making and help you navigate complex financial challenges with confidence.

## Creating an Account

> Creating an account for yourself is easy, it only takes a few steps!

On the main page, locate and click the *"Register"* button in the bottom-left corner. This will take you to a role selection screen. If you are an individual investor, select the *"Individual"* option and proceed to enter the required information.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/individual.png" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>

After completing the registration process, you will be redirected to the login page. Here, you can enter your credentials to access your account.

### Upload and Download the Accounts

> Before uploading the files, ensure that there are no blank lines in the Excel sheet. This will help prevent any errors during the upload process.

After login, you will have a interface that looks like below:

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/maininterface_individual.png" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>

Click on the red button located in the sidebar. 

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/sidebar.png" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>

This will direct you to a page where you can upload the necessary data.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/upload_individual.png" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>

**First**, click on the *“Download Investment Data”* button. This will provide you with a template in the correct format for recording the necessary data:

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/wealthdata.png" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>

**Next**, please complete the following fields in the investment data, leaving the *"Account Number"* field blank, but with no blank lines:

-   **Investment Type**: Select the type of investment (choose one: Stock, Bond, Fund, or ETF).
-   **Investment Symbol**: Provide the ticker symbol or identifier for the investment (e.g., "AAPL").
-   **Investment Name**: Enter the name of the investment (e.g., "Apple Inc.").
-   **Units Held**: Input the number of units or shares held.
-   **Purchase Price**: Specify the price per unit or share at the time of purchase.
-   **Purchase Date**: Record the date the investment was purchased.
-   **Last Traded Price**: Enter the most recent traded price per unit or share.

Once you've completed the steps above, the account information will be updated. You can verify the details by clicking on the first button in the sidebar. You should see something shown below:

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/interface_indi.png" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>
